The Problem

Today's satellites are dumb terminals.

They wait for ground commands. They waste energy in overly conservative safety zones. They can't adapt to what they encounter in orbit.

In a world of thousands of CubeSats, that doesn't scale.

Satellites should be smarter than that.

  • 01
    Energy waste
    Rule-based systems are too conservative with battery and thermal limits.
  • 02
    Operational slowness
    Heavy dependence on ground stations, even for simple maneuvers.
  • 03
    Limited endurance
    Small form factors struggle with energy-hungry ion thrusters.
[ 02 / 05 ]
The Vision

A new generation of autonomous satellites.

ExplorAetern develops onboard intelligence for next-generation CubeSats. Our technology enables satellites to make their own decisions — saving energy, accelerating missions, and reducing ground dependency.

The foundation is built. The patent is filed. The future is in orbit.
