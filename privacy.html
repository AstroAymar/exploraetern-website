Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ExplorAetern ("we", "us", "our") — a research project led by Aymar Suery, operating as a registered sole proprietor in Morocco — collects, uses, and protects information through this website (exploraetern.com).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the personal information you voluntarily provide when you contact us directly via email or schedule a call through our booking link. This typically includes your name, email address, and any information you choose to share in your message.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services that collect identifiable personal data on this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you share is used solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule conversations, and maintain professional correspondence. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

This website may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

— Vercel (hosting infrastructure)
— Zoho Mail (email correspondence)
— Calendly (call scheduling)

4. Data Retention

We retain correspondence only as long as necessary for legitimate professional purposes. You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at aymar@exploraetern.com.

6. International Visitors

If you are visiting from the European Union, the United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR, UK-GDPR, or CCPA respectively. We honor all such requests in good faith.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email aymar@exploraetern.com.
